                              How To Play:

                      - Press (r) to roll the dice.
                  - Press (h) to hold the current score.

                                 Rules:

      - If you roll double six twice in a row then your global score will be reset.
              - If you roll one then your current score will be reset.
      - If one of your score resets then the turn will pass on to the next player.

                      Press any key to skip rules.
